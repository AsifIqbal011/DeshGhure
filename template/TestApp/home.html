Sundarbans: world's largest mangrove forest and a UNESCO World Heritage Site. This unique ecosystem is home to the Royal Bengal Tiger, spotted deer, crocodiles, and a diverse array of bird species. The forest is characterized by a complex network of tidal waterways, mudflats, and small islands, making it a unique and vital ecosystem.
Cox's Bazar: The coastal city in southeastern Bangladesh, renowned for its 120-kilometer-long unbroken sandy beach, the world's longest natural sea beach. This popular tourist destination offers attractions like Himchari National Park, Inani Beach, and Dulahazara Safari Park.
